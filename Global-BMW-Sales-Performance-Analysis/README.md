🚗 Global BMW Vehicle Sales Dataset (Simulated)
📊 Overview

This dataset contains simulated global BMW vehicle sales data enriched with key market, economic and operational variables across multiple countries and years. It is designed for data analysis, visualisation and machine learning applications related to automotive sales performance.

The dataset enables users to explore how pricing, marketing investment, dealership presence and macroeconomic conditions influence vehicle sales outcomes.

📁 Dataset Features

The dataset includes the following categories of variables:

🔢 Sales & Product Information

units_sold – Number of vehicles sold (Target Variable)

model – BMW model type

segment – Vehicle segment (SUV, Sedan, etc.)

engine_type – Petrol, Diesel, Hybrid, Electric

💰 Pricing & Marketing

price_usd – Vehicle price

marketing_spend_usd – Marketing investment

🏢 Operational Factors

dealership_count – Number of dealerships in region

competition_index – Market competition intensity

🌍 Geographic & Time

country – Country of sale

year, month – Time indicators

📈 Economic Indicators

fuel_price_usd – Fuel price

gdp_growth_percent – GDP growth rate

interest_rate_percent – Interest rate

This dataset is well-suited for:
